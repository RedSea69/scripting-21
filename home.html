About Our Home

The city of Lethbridge, home of around 100,000 people located near the Canadian Rocky Mountains, it's a place of warmth and comfort, not only by the help of the Rocky Mountains but also by its strong and abled community.

Who can stay strong and warm even throughout the winter. The city holds a very long history that dates back to the 19th century. Lethbridge has been through a lot, but just like the many generations that came before us, it stands as a symbol of great Albertan might, the beautiful, powerful production site of Southern Alberta!
